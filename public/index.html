Hotel Jinetes de Osa

Come see Costa Rica as you always imagined; wild, beautiful and unexplored. Be one of the lucky few to scuba dive in the species rich waters off Caño Island or explore one of the last remaining virgin lowland tropical rainforests of Corcovado National Park. Splendidly isolated in the tiny community of Drake Bay, Costa Rica, Hotel Jinetes De Osa is a friendly beachside retreat one hours boat ride down the jungle lined banks of the Sierpe River. Upon arrival, you can immediately enjoy secluded beaches, deserted waterfalls and a walk among one of the largest nesting populations of Scarlet Macaws in Costa Rica. So leave the hectic pace of the modern world behind and come explore with us at the Jinetes De Osa Resort, Drake Bay, Costa Rica.

Rainforest Activities

Canopy Tour
See the rainforest from a unique perspective. Zip from tree to tree in search of animals, birds and fun!
Corcovado National Park
Crowned "the most biologically intense places on earth" by National Geographic Magazine, Corcovado National Park offers excellent opportunities to hike and see wildlife.
Horseback Riding
Take a trot along the beach or a gallop through the rainforest.
Bird Watching
Hundreds of species birds in the area make Drake Bay and the Osa Peninsula a birdwatcher's paradise.
Waterfall Hike
With several spectacular waterfalls in the area you can take a relaxing or romantic dip.

Ocean Activities

Dolphin and Whale Encounters
Come watch huge pods of dolphins and whales play. This is an experience you will never forget.
Snorkeling
Go snorkeling at Caño Island which has some of the best snorkeling in Costa Rica. You will see several different species of fish and coral.
Sea Kayaking
Take a coastline tour of Drake Bay with several jungle-lined rivers to explore.
Scuba Diving
Drake Bay's oldest and most respected PADI dive facility. Costa Rica Adventure Divers.
Sport Fishing
Some of the best and most exciting sport fishing in the world is found in this region.
